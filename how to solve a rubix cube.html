Your steps to solving the rubix cube

follow these steps and you will solve the rubix cube in no time!

Step 1: The white daisy

all you have to do in this step is to form a flower shaped pattern on the top or the one with the yellow middle and the white edged pieces facing upwards.

you can do this by positioning the white edge pieces on the top

Step 2: The white cross

This step is also pretty easy... position the flower such that one of the white edge pieces fit with the color of the middle piece.

after you did that, turn the face 2 times, repeat with all the other edges.

Step 3: The first layer

now here is where you will need to learn an algorithm. to those who think this step is easy, skip to the next step.

to those who dont know how, place the corner piece above the place where it should be and use this algorithm,

R|U|R'|U'

or if you want a more faster way of doing this, You can also:

R'|F|R|F'

note that this is according to my personal opinion. just because its easy for me it doesnt mean the same for you.

Step 4: The second layer

This step is not that hard, but if you really ponder upon it for some time, you will understand it. Anyways here are the steps:

select an edge piece and turn the top until it matches with a middle piece.

then check whether if you the piece should be fitted to the left or right side, if the piece should go to the right, then

U|R|U'|R'|F|R'|F'|R

If it should go to the left, then:

U'|L'|U|L|F'|L'|F|L

Step 5: The yellow cross

from now on, most of these, if not all of these next steps are all algorithms. so get ready and practice!

there are 3 possibilities. either you have no edges facing up, or you have 2 edges facing up, or you already have the cross. if you do then skip this section, if you dont, read on.

possibility 1: two edges facing up

now there are two possibilities again. the first one being that the two edges maybe directly opposite or next to each other

for the edges being opposite each other, use this algorithm

F|R|U|R'|U'

and for the edges being next to each other, position the top such that one of the edges is facing you and the other facing right, then use this algorithm

F^2|R|U|R'|U'

possibility 2: no edges facing up

take any side and use this algorithm:

F|R|U|R'|U'

then use this algorithm:

F^2|R|U|R'|U'

now you might be wondering, why i used "^2". thats because i meant that you should turn the front along with the middle layer behind the front. now if you didnt uderstand what i said go to contacts.html
